Dataset 2: National Survey of Substance Abuse Treatment Services

Dataset link: https://www.datafiles.samhsa.gov/study-series/national-survey-substance-abuse-treatment-services-n-ssats-nid13519


Dataset description: This dataset is an annual census that collects information on facilities that provide substance abuse treatment from all 50 states. This includes both public and private facilities. This study collects three types of information:  characteristics of individual facilities (types of services offered and treatment provided), information and counts on clients, and general information about the facility. 

What we're looking for: We hope to see how the prevalence of substance abuse services have changed over the years. We can also look for various trends among different types of facilities.

Dataset: National Mental Health Services Survey

Dataset Link: https://www.datafiles.samhsa.gov/study-series/national-mental-health-services-survey-n-mhss-nid13521

Dataset Description: The National Mental Health Services Survey provides information on how mental health treatment facilities operate in all 50 states of the United States. The types of mental health facilities in this study include psychiatric hospitals, outpatient mental health facilities, residential treatment centers, community mental health centers and mostly all other mental health facilities only excluding private practitioners, jails, and military treatment facilities. Starting from 2014, the survey also gives us statistics on the demographics of the people who attend these facilities so we are able to see the characteristics of the patients. Our dataset contains this information for the years 2010, 2012, 2014, 2015, 2016, 2017, and 2018. Some of the variables studied each year include facility type, primary treatment focus of facility, whether or not the facility provides specific treatments, and numerous others.
